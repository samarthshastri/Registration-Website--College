Welcome to kalanjali '12

Sir MVIT, Bengaluru has been conducting Kalanjali an annual intercollegiate cultural extravaganza successfully since the year 1996. Kalanjali has been setting the stage for student community to showcase their talents, innovations, creative zeal and zest. Kalanjali '12 is scheduled to be held on 28th and 29th of September 2012
