technical events

The technical events in Kalanjali '12 are as follows:


IT Boooom" : software presentation

  • Maximum of 4 participants per team.
  • Must be a self developed software.
  • Multimedia based presentation is allowed.
  • No voice data will be accepted, except if it is a part of project procedures.
  • Time limit is 10 + 2 mins.
  • The last 2 mins are for questions from the audience/judges.
  • DLP/DHP will be provided.
  • Only one computer with sound card and CD-ROM drive will be provided for DP presentation.


"Keep guessing" : General Quiz

  • 2 Participants per team.
  • The event consists of preliminary round (written) and the team will be shortlisted for final round (oral).
  • The specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to give a particular answer and type of round will be informed before the
    commencement of the event
  • Questions will be based on general topics, technical, scientific and current technologies.


"Tech views" : Technical Paper Presentation

  • Individual event
  • Any popular scientific or technical topic, irrespective of the branch can be chosen
  • The time limit is 10 + 2 mins. Last 2 mins are for questions from audience/judges
  • DLP/DHP will be provided.
  • Multimedia based presentation is allowed.
  • There will be no commitment to provide additional / supporting facilities or equipment on the part of the organizers.
    Candidates must furnish them on their own.
  • Decision on the judges will be final.



"ctr-f9" : coding and debugging

  • 2 participants per team.
  • Any number of teams per college.
  • Coding language to be in C or C++.
  • 2 rounds – (First round)- (manual) - participants have to debug a program in C++ on paper.
  • (Second round)-(On comp) : selected participants to code on the computer.
  • Any kind of malpractice will result in disqualification of the team.
  • Event regestration fee : Rs.80
  • Registration to be closed at 1pm on the day of event.


"Innovate" : Model making

  • 2 participants per team.
  • Materials are provided : (Eg. Straws, toothpicks, pins, glue) etc..
  • Judgement is based on stability, aesthetics and creativity.
  • Time Limit : 1 hour.
