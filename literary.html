Literary events

The Literal events in Kalanjali '12 are as follows:


"Keep guessing" : General Quiz

  • 2 Participants per team.
  • The event consists of preliminary round (written) and the team will be shortlisted for final round (oral).
  • The specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to give a particular answer and type of round will be informed before the
    commencement of the event
  • Questions will be based on general topics, technical, scientific and current technologies.


    • "Speak your thoughts" : Debate

  • Participants can speak 'for' or 'against' the topic.
  • Medium or expression will be English, Hindi or Kannada.
  • Paper reading is not allowed.
  • Duration is 4 + 1 mins.
  • Topic for the competition will be announced 24 hours prior to the start of program (For topic confirmation contact student convener on 28th
    sep 2012, 11:00 am onwards).


    • "Pen It Down" : Essay Writing

  • The medium of expression can be English, Hindi or Kannada.
  • Topic will be given on the spot.
  • Duration is 60 mins.


    • "Soothing Odes" : Poetry Recitation

  • Duration is 4 + 1 mins.
  • Topic will be given 30 min before the start of the event.
  • Language can be English, Hindi or Kannada.
  • The participants would be required to submit the content of the poem in writing to the organizers at the time of recitaion.
