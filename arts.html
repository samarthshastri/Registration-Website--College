Fine Arts

The Fine Arts events in Kalanjali '12 are as follows:


"Catch it on canvas" : On Spot Painting

  • Event will be conducted on the spot and participants will be requested to do painting on the subject given.
  • Duration is 2 hours.
  • Painting can be done in oil, water, poster or pastel colours.
  • Candidates shall bring their own materials like brushes, paints etc. Only the drawing sheet will be provided by the college.

    • "Not so funny" : Cartooning

  • Event will be conducted on spot on the given subject or idea.
  • Duration is 2 hours.
  • Participants will bring their own materials and college will provide the drawing paper.

    • "Indian floor art" : Rangoli

  • Duration is 2 hours.
  • Participants will bring their own materials. This art is known differently in various regions such as Mandna, Alpana, Alekhan, Rangoli etc.
    For this the medium and form of expression can be free hand, pictorial or descriptive.
  • Only one medium shall be used - colour powder or flower petals or saw dust or rice without pasting.
  • The participants will have to prepare a Rangoli within the space provided by the organizers.


    • "ctrl-X ctrl-V" : Collage

  • Event will be conducted on spot on the given topic or subject.
  • Duration is 2 hours.
  • The college will provide the drawing paper.
  • Participants will bring their own pasting and other materials required for the contest.
  • A collage has to be prepared from old magazines.
  • Team can have 2+1 participants.

    • "Clix" : Spot photography

  • Individual Event
  • Topic will be given on day 1.
  • Camera with resolution 8MP and above are to be used.
  • Photographs should be taken within the campus only.
  • A maximum of 5 best photos to be submitted
  • Registration fees : Rs100 per entry.
