Dramatics

The various events under this category in Kalanjali '12 are as follows:


"c'mon X-press" : Mime

  • Team can consist of 6 + 2 members.
  • Duration is 6 + 2 mins.
  • The evaluation is based on the theme, acting, creativity, makeup, music and general impression.
  • Props are not allowed.

    • "See what we mean" : Dumb Charades

  • Three members per team
  • No lip-sync allowed
  • Referring through color, names is not allowed
  • Should not break a word more than twice
  • Max 3 mins to guess the answer

    • "On the streets" : Street Play

  • Team can consist of 8 + 2 members
  • Duration 10 + 2 mins
  • Play can be in English, Hindi or Kannada
  • No props allowed other than dupattas, head/wrist bands
  • No pre-recorded music allowed
  • General rules of street play are applicable
