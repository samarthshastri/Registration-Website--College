Dance

The dance events in Kalanjali '12 are as follows:


"Natya Bhangi" : CLASSICAL DANCE SOLO

  • Duration is 8 + 4 mins.
  • The classical dance can be from any of the approved form of dances such as Kathak, Kathakkali, Bharathanatyam, Manipuri, Kuchipudi, Mohiniyattam, Odissi etc.
  • Recorded music is allowed.
  • Evaluation will be based on taal, technique, rhythm, abhinaya, costume, footwork and general impression.
    •

    "X-wood jatkas" : GROUP DANCE (FILMY)

  • Duration is 6 + 4 mins.
  • Group can consist of 6 + 4 members.
  • Dance can be Filmy, Contemporary or Western style.
  • Recorded music is allowed.
  • Evaluation will be based on rhythm, formation, expression, costume, makeup, sets and overall effects.
    •

    "Boogie Woogie" : FILMY DANCE SOLO

  • Duration is 5 + 2 mins.
  • Only filmy dance is allowed in this item.
  • Recorded music is allowed.
  • Evaluation will be based on Taal, technique, rhythm, Abhinaya, costume, footwork and general impression.
    •

    "Innovative creations" : CLASSICAL / CREATIVE/FOLK GROUP DANCE (Non - Filmy)

  • Duration is 10 mins.
  • Time for sets and instrument settings is only 5 mins.
  • Dance is strictly classical/creative or Folk. No film songs are entertained.
  • Recorded music is allowed.
  • Evaluation will be based on rhythm, formation, expression, costumes, makeup, sets and overall effects.
